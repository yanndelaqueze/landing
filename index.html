Top 3 trail races

UTMB

The UTMB Mont-Blanc is the most incredible trail running event! Each year, the best athletes meet in Chamonix and more than 10'000 runners participate in one of the event's 8 races, including the UTMB World Series Finals.

171km / 10,000m D+

Grand Raid de la Réunion

The Grand Raid de la Réunion, also called "La diagonale des fous" is a mountain ultramarathon race. The race takes place annually in October on Réunion island

162km / 9,600m D+

Hardrock 100

The Hardrock Hundred Mile Endurance Run is an ultramarathon held on a loop course in Southern Colorado, US. The race is dedicated to the memory of the miners who settled in the area and who built the mining trails on which much of the race is run.

161km / 10,000m D+
